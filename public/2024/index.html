Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the event?
Registration is conducted online. You can reserve your spot by filling out the registration form on our event page.
Are food and beverage services will be provided during events?
Various snacks and beverages will be available throughout the event. If you have specific dietary requirements, please inform us in advance.
Is there a refund policy in case I need to cancel my attendance?
Unfortunately, no, there isn't a refund policy for cancellations.
Where can I find the latest information about this event?
You can find the latest information about this event right here on our social page! We'll be posting updates regularly.
What should I bring on the day of the event?
Ensure to bring your registration confirmation and identification. If you have any specific inquiries, please contact us.
Is the venue accessible for wheelchair users?
The main event is on the 2nd floor (not wheelchair accessible). However, we offer a first-floor room with live streaming for attendees with disabilities.

Get in touch with us

See the event update on our social media platforms and contact us if you have any questions.
